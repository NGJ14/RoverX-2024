About RoverX

We are a group of young, enthusiastic individuals who build Mars rovers by the year and compete at various platforms.

Our prototype is used to compete in different international competitions including IRC (International Rover Challenege), URC (University Rover Challenge), ERC (European Rover Challenge) and ARC (Anatolian Rover Challenge). These competitions provide a platform for student-run teams from all over the globe to showcase their rovers.

Working on our rover exposes us to various other practical and theoretical disciplines of engineering, thereby providing depth to our knowledge. As a team, we begin to understand the work culture in industries and problem-solving in real-time, while following a timeline based approach.
